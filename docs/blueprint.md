# **App Name**: Listening to Echoes

## Core Features:

- User Story Submission: Allow users to securely submit personal recollections (written or audio) about educational experiences, ensuring data privacy is respected from the point of entry.
- Librarian Content Review Tool: Provide a private interface for librarians to review submitted content, flag potentially revealing personal details using an AI tool, and approve entries for the public archive.
- Public Story Archive Display: Present approved stories in a browsable archive, allowing users to read or listen to recollections. Crucially, this archive will explicitly lack any social features (sharing, liking, commenting).
- Metadata Tagging and Trending: An AI-powered tool that analyzes the submitted stories to identify and suggest thematic tags and detect trending topics or keywords for categorization and for authors to use.
- Author Content Creation: Enable designated authors and co-authors to create and publish written articles or audio podcasts, primarily based on the trending metadata and themes identified in the archive.
- Search & Filter Archive: Implement a robust search and filtering system to allow users to easily find stories by keywords, themes, or metadata within the public archive.
- Support Resources Directory: Dedicated section providing critical information for mental and emotional support, including national hotlines, support groups, and relevant organizations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Drawing inspiration from the reflective nature of a digital archive and the depth of human experience, a dark color scheme creates an inviting, introspective environment. The primary color is a muted deep blue (#456273), chosen for its contemplative qualities. The background color is a heavily desaturated dark blue (#1E2226) to provide a serene canvas that allows content to stand out. An accent color of brighter cyan (#68C9DB) provides clear contrast for interactive elements and highlights.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, structured feel suitable for titles and navigation. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to ensure high readability and neutrality for personal narratives and longer textual content.
- Use minimalist, line-based icons throughout the application to maintain a clean aesthetic that supports functionality without distracting from the user-generated content. Icons should be clear and universally understandable.
- The layout should be clean, content-focused, and organized with ample whitespace to enhance readability and encourage reflection. Utilize a grid or card-based structure for archive entries, ensuring consistency across different content types.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for navigation and content loading. Animations should be unobtrusive, reinforcing a sense of calm and professionalism without drawing excessive attention to themselves, such as soft fades or gentle slides.
